About Masco.

Founded in 2020, Masco. is a leader in innovative technology solutions. Our mission is to create cutting-edge gadgets that simplify and enhance everyday life.

Our team of engineers and designers work tirelessly to push the boundaries of what’s possible, from AI-powered home devices to eco-friendly drones. We believe in sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction.
